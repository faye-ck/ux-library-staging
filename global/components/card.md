# Card

> **Category:** Surfaces. Contained surface for grouped content — combines image, text, and actions.

## Variants
- Elevated (default, with shadow)
- Outlined (border, no shadow)

## Sizes
- Adapts to content or fixed

## States
Default · Hover (with CardActionArea)

## Colors
Default (surface background)

## Key Options
- CardHeader — title, subtitle, avatar, and action
- CardMedia — image or video
- CardContent — body text
- CardActions — footer button area
- CardActionArea — full-card clickable overlay
- Expandable card — Collapse + accordion pattern

## Use
- Product cards
- Article previews
- Team member profiles
- Dashboard widgets

## Customization
- Styling and theme overrides: product `_theme.md`
- Component-level usage rules: product `_components.md`
