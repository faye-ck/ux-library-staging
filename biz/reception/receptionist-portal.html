Security Card Management — Receptionist Portal

Inherits card management from admin portal (sections 1–3). This page covers guest check-in/out flows only. Admin Portal setup →

4.1 — Check-in Guests

Card reader activates automatically at check-in. Success: check-in time = card assign time.

Skip Card: Reason required → "Continue without card". Reason recorded as In: prefix in Status Remark. "Scan card" available later for different assign time.

Bulk check-in: Queue item labelled "Skipped" when card skipped.

4.2 — Check-out Guests

Card reader activates only if "Also activate during check-out" is enabled. Success: check-out time = card return time.

  • Lost/Damaged: must reactivate before returning.

  • Guests with no assigned card: card reader not activated at check-out.
  • Bulk check-out (mixed): cardless guests checked out immediately; guests with cards queued for scanning.
