Seat/Space Analytics Dashboard

Historical usage analysis — space and seat utilization, no-show rates, check-in patterns by department.

1 — Layout

Main Menu

Analytics menu item, placed after Locker.

Global Filters

Time period: This week (default) · Last week · This/Last month · This/Last quarter · This/Last year · Next week/month · Custom range (max 31 days, 5-month window).

Location: All area groups (default) → area group → office map. Apply button enabled only on change. "Reset to default" shown after applying.

Sub-menu (Phase 1)

  • Space Utilization
  • Seat Utilization
  • Check-in by Department

Time Dimension Toggle

PeriodAvailable Dimensions
This/Last weekHour · Day · Day of Week
This/Last monthHour · Day · Day of Week · Week · Month
This/Last quarterHour · Day of Week · Week · Month · Quarter
This/Last yearHour · Day of Week · Week · Month · Quarter · Year

Holidays and non-working days excluded (tooltip). Data outside business hours (space) or time slots (seat) excluded.

2 — Space Utilization Dashboard

Figma →

Stacked bar graph: Utilized · No-show · Not utilized. Comparison checkbox = double x-axis. Summary: Utilization rate % · Meeting time / Available time (hrs) · No-show count · No-show rate %. Data table with pagination (10/25/50) and CSV export. PDF export in A4 format.

3 — Seat Utilization Dashboard

Figma →

Stacked bar for utilization. Grid table (Time Utilization Breakdown) for day × hour view. Color coding:

Group Seats with Unlimited Capacity: line + stacked area chart measuring usage count.

4 — Business Hours Setting

Location: Admin → Settings → General Settings → Business Hours (after Store Time Zone). Toggle (default: Off). When on: Start + End time (required, default 09:00–18:00). Affects Space Utilization only.

5 — Check-in by Department

Moved to Analytics sub-menu. Filters updated to global filter system. UI updated for consistency with new dashboards.
